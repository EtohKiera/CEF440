How to Get the Google Maps URL for your Store

  1. Open Google Maps in your web browser.
  2. Search or Move to the desired location using the search bar at the top of the page.
  3. Once the location appears on the map, right-click on the location or its marker.
  4. A context menu will appear. Click on "What's here?" in the context menu.
  5. A small pop-up window will appear at the bottom of the page with the latitude and longitude coordinates of the location.
  6. Click on the coordinates in the pop-up window.
  7. The URL for the location will appear in the search bar at the top of the page.
  8. Copy the URL from the search bar. It will be in the format "https://www.google.com/maps/place/LATITUDE,LONGITUDE".

That's it! You now have the Google Maps URL for the desired location. You can copy the link and use it to Add the Store .
